Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my personal information? +
You can edit your profile information by clicking the "Edit" button on your profile card. After making changes, click "Save" to update your information.
How do I schedule an appointment? +
Navigate to the "Appointments" section and click on a doctor's profile. You'll see options to schedule either an in-person or virtual appointment.
How do I create an account? +
Click on "Sign Up" at the top right corner and follow the steps.
Is my medical information secure? +
Yes, we use encryption and comply with HIPAA/GDPR to protect your data.
Can I reschedule an appointment? +
Yes, log in and go to "My Appointments" to modify bookings.
